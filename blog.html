Introduction to Blog?

A person is often seen asking, what is a blog ? Here, comes the answer. A blog is a kind of webpage or website which can be used for discussion or getting information about a particular topic or subject. Generally, a blog is an online diary where one can keep his data or information updated regularly or he/she can make changes to any part of a blog.
